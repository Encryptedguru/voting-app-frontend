About VoteBcC

VoteBC is a web app that allow netcitizens of Kenya to make decisions on their political leaders using e-voting technology to enhace free and fair elections organized.

This web app is an inspiration by Kenya 2022 general elections, which resulted to hiking of prices of basic commodites like unga to fund Ksh 40.9 Billion election budget.
